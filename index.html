Jewelry Knowledge

After Care

After you get your piercing it's best to keep it clean so it doesn't get infected, the best thing to use is saline solution.

Don't Pierce at Home

If you want a piercing then you should never do it at home, you shouldn't use a sewing needle because your ear can get infected it is best to go to a professional.

Rejection

Sometimes your body can reject the jewelry and can get very red and infected. The skin can get thin and tear if your body is rejecting the jewelry.
